Harnessing the powerhouse of the cell to cure type 2 diabetes

Science

Increasing mitochondrial metabolism corrects the energy balance and reverses type 2 diabetes. We are working on a drug that activates mitochondrial metabolism to burn calories without exercise which allows to reverse the type 2 diabetes epidemic.

UCSF professor Yuriy Kirichok, the world's leading expert in mitochondrial heat production and cofounder of Equator Therapeutics, recently discovered the biological pathway that controls resting metabolic rate. This discovery paves the road to developing the first safe drug to increase metabolic rate and burn calories without exercise.

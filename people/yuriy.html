Yuriy Kirichok
CSO
Yuriy is a Professor at the University of California San Francisco (UCSF). He is a world leading expert in biophysics and physiology of cellular membranes. Yuriy has pushed the envelope in his studies of electrical currents controlling fundamental physiological functions and made several breakthroughs in the fields of bioenergetics and fertilization. These discoveries have been published in top scientific journals including Nature and Cell. Yuriy pioneered methods to measure the tiny electrical currents generated by mitochondria - the powerhouse of the cell. He used these methods to discovery the native physiological mechanisms that mitochondria use to generate heat, laying down the essential foundation for the technology developed by Equator Therapeutics.
